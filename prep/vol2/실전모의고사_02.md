## ── 정답 ──

### A1. Q1 (Oracle · 검증됨)
```sql
SELECT member_id, total, rnk
FROM (
    SELECT o.member_id,
           SUM(oi.quantity * p.price)                              AS total,
           RANK() OVER (ORDER BY SUM(oi.quantity * p.price) DESC)  AS rnk
    FROM ORDERS o
    JOIN ORDER_ITEM oi ON o.order_id   = oi.order_id
    JOIN PRODUCT p     ON oi.product_id = p.product_id
    WHERE o.status = '완료'
    GROUP BY o.member_id
)
WHERE rnk <= 3
ORDER BY rnk;
```
- 결과: 이영희(1,663,000·1위), 김철수(1,416,000·2위), 박민수(153,000·3위).
- 포인트: **집계(SUM) 위에 분석함수(RANK)** → 서브쿼리로 감싸 `rnk<=3`. 상세 → [sql_심화_01](sql_심화_01_분석함수.md).
